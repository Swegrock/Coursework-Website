Projects
For my third year project I have several ideas, all of which I'll be considering over the next year. I want to pick a project that best showcases my skills I've learnt.

Generating Meat For 3D Printing
A new invention which has recently come to light is 3D printed meat, here's a video of it in action. They use a handful of Steak models with all of the fats and meat meticulously placed to recreate the real thing. The problem is this doesn't allow for the steaks to appear unique as they all look very similar, my project would be to write some software which could generate realistic looking meat while still maintaining a high standard.

Photo app for travelers
When traveling around the globe, many travelers take photos of the places they visit and the sights they see. My second project idea is to create an app which will use machine learning and other methods to rate the best photos of each monument based on current trends surrounding the monument taken from other social networks such as instagram and facebook. Each use would get an overall score for their photos and the incentive would be to reach the top of the global and national leaderboards.

Investigating family dynamics using Machine Learning
Using machine learning we can simulate all sorts of different behaviors much like they'd be formed in the real world. I want to be able to simulate family dynamics in the traditional sense, as would be seen with animals in the wild. I'd hopefully simulate feeding, protection and teaching in a simplistic manner. The MLAgents in Unity would be a good platform to use as it allows me to use reinforcement learning to help develop the behavior.
